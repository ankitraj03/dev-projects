All that you need to know about Woodpecker’s Hackathon

Woodpecker Analytics & Services is excited to host a Virtual Hackathon, inviting innovative thinkers to tackle real-world challenges. Participants will have the opportunity to showcase their creativity and problem-solving skills while competing for a prize pool of Rs. 1 Lakh.

This event is a unique chance to:

  • Gain hands-on experience by working on practical, impactful problems.
  • Receive mentorship and feedback from seasoned professionals.
  • Enhance your portfolio with a functional prototype developed during the hackathon.

Join us and bring your innovative ideas to life!

Problem Statements will be visible after Registration.

Eligibility & Rules

  • All students and working professionals are eligible to participate.
  • Each team can comprise 1 to 3 members, fostering a collaborative environment.
  • Each participant is restricted to membership in only one team.
  • Participants are required to use their own systems and are free to utilize any software tools.
  • Any team found engaging in unethical practices will be disqualified.
  • Teams cannot be changed after registration.

Submission Guidelines

Round 1:

  • Each registered team can select a problem statement and develop an approach to solve it within the specified domain.
  • Submit your approach as a presentation in PPT/PPTX format along with a 2-minute explanatory video.
  • File name format: Teamname_round1.ppt/pptx

Round 2:

  • Shortlisted teams will need to develop a working prototype.
  • Submit the code, output samples, and a 3-minute video showcasing the prototype as a zip file.
  • The video file should not exceed 200MB.
  • Later, teams will be allocated a 10-minute online slot to demonstrate the prototype live.

Judging Criteria:

  • Functionality
  • Innovation and Creativity
  • Implementation
  • Scalability and Performance
  • Documentation

Terms and Conditions:

  • Woodpecker Analytics and Services will have complete intellectual property (IP) rights for all submissions.
  • Internship offers will be provided to deserving candidates based on their performance and not solely on their rank.
  • Prize money awarded to the winners will have GST deducted before being sent.
  • Participants must align their problem statements with the theme and industry provided in the gallery.
